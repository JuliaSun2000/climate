About us

We are the official committee representing the government of Planet Kitten in dealing with the problem of climate change on Earth impacting the entire universe, with approval and cooperation of the United Nations.


Our Values

In the past years, climate change on Earth has significantly affecting the environment of the solar system by disrupting the regular movement of cosmic particles, which has led to the redirection of galactic current that is menacing Planet Kitten’s ecosystem. Our responsibility is to conduct detailed research on causes of this problem and design a feasible method to resolve it.
